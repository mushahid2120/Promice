What is Pro-Mice?

In today's fast-paced world, staying organized can be a challenge. Pro Mice is an innovative application designed to consolidate your saved and bookmarked content from multiple social media platforms into one centralized and easily accessible place.

With Pro-Mice, you can achieve following:-

Save Time

Enhance Productivity

Never lose track of Important Information again.

Key Features

1) Unified Content Management

Save and access content from Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, and more, all in one place.

2) Smart Search

Find your saved content effortlessly with our powerful keyword search feature.

3) Automatic Transcripts

Get text versions of your saved videos and posts for quick reference and easy reading.

4) Cross-Platform Sync

Sync your saved content across all your devices seamlessly.

5) Notifications and Reminders

Receive personalized notifications and reminders about your saved content.

How It Works

  1. Sign up and link your social media accounts. Get started by creating an account and linking your social media profiles to Pro Mice.
  2. Save content with a single click. Use the Pro Mice extension or app to save content from any platform instantly, whether it's a post, video, or article.
  3. Access and search your saved content. Easily find your saved posts, videos, and articles with our smart search feature, which uses advanced keyword search capabilities.
  4. Enjoy personalized notifications. Receive reminders and notifications about your saved content at the right time, ensuring you never miss important information.
